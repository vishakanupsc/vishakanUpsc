About Us

The driving force behind our students' success.

R. VISHAKAN

B.E., M.Sc. (Psychology)

Founder-Director

With over 7 years of dedicated teaching experience, our Founder R. Vishakan, a UGC-NET Qualified faculty member, is a trusted mentor for serious UPSC aspirants. Having himself successfully cleared multiple UPSC Mains examinations and appeared for the UPSC Interview, and having guided numerous aspirants through their preparation journey, he brings rare, first-hand insight into handling every stage of the Civil Services Examination—from the Prelims race pressure, to the Mains marathon stress, and finally to the calm composure required for the Interview personality test.

His unique approach of blending humour with core UPSC concepts while simplifying complex topics is a hallmark of his teaching style, making learning both effective and engaging. This approach goes beyond mere content mastery to focus on mental resilience, exam temperament, consistency, and decision-making under pressure—often the decisive factors in UPSC success.
